How to Delete Your Procrastino Account

Procrastino allows users to permanently delete their account directly from within the app.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Procrastino app.
  2. Go to the Settings page.
  3. Tap Manage Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion in the popup dialog.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data are permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.
